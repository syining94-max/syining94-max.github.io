# Malab

**Intelligent Synthetic Biology Laboratory, School of Biotechnology, Jiangnan University**  
**江南大学生物工程学院智能合成生物学实验室**

Malab focuses on intelligent synthetic biology, integrating biology, engineering, and computational approaches to understand and design biological systems.

## Research Keywords

- Synthetic Biology
- Intelligent Biotechnology
- Bioengineering
- Computational Biology

## Introduction

We are interested in building and understanding biological systems through interdisciplinary approaches that combine experimental biology, engineering, and computational methods.
